What We Believe

We believe in God the Father Almighty, creator of heaven and earth. We believe in Jesus Christ, his Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the virgin Mary.

He suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died and was buried; he descended to hell. The third day he rose again from the dead. He ascended to heaven and is seated at the right hand of God the Father Almighty.

From there he will come to judge the living and the dead. We believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and the life everlasting. Amen.

Our Mission Statement: We exist to share the message of Christ Jesus and to be faithful with the talents He has given us by pointing to the cross.
